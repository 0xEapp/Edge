Help - Troubleshooting

Video not playing

The app uses your mobile internet connection to access the app and the video content. If you are having trouble viewing the videos, please turn on your wifi connection to help stream the videos.

We have set the videos to high resolution. This will require longer loading times based on the strength of your internet connection. If your internet connection is not strong enough the video will play at a lower resolution.
